The Caribbean

Known for sunny skies, clear water, and sandy beaches, the Caribbean is a region of the Americas that comprises the Caribbean Sea, its surrounding coasts, and its islands. The region lies southeast of the Gulf of Mexico and of the North American mainland, east of Central America, and north of South America. If you are dying for an unforgettable weekend at the beach, take a trip to the Caribbean!





New York City

Bustling with night life, bright lights, and high rises, New York City is the most populous city in the United States. It is also the financial capital of the U.S., as it is home to the nation's stock market and Wall Street. If you want to see famous sights like the Statue of Liberty, or have an adventure in the city that never sleeps, take a trip to New York City!





Amsterdam

A cultural and financial capital of the Netherlands and all of Europe, Amsterdam is a city rich with history and novel experiences. If you book a trip to Amsterdam, make sure you check out the historic canals, Van Gogh Museum, and if you're old enough, the red-light district!





Japan

Japan is one of Earth's most densely populated and urbanized nation's, and also produces some of the most recognizable technology, art, and cuisine. If you want hop around some of Japan's 6852 islands, experience the majesty of Mount Fuji, or just soak in the lights of Tokyo, book your trip to Japan today!
